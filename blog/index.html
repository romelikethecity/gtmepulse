Why We Write These

Salary pages give you numbers. Career guides give you frameworks. Blog articles give you arguments. Each piece starts with a single data point from the report and asks: what does this mean for your career, your hiring process, or your negotiation strategy?

We take positions. The data is the foundation, but the interpretation is editorial. When 68% of GTM Engineers have no meaningful equity, that's a data point. When we argue that companies are systematically undervaluing the role by treating builders like operators, that's a thesis you can agree or disagree with.

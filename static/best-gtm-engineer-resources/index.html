Best Resources for GTM Engineers in 2026

The GTM Engineer role barely existed three years ago. Now there are 3,000+ open positions and a growing ecosystem of newsletters, tools, and communities built specifically for this audience. The problem is finding what's worth your time.

We spent weeks talking to practitioners, reading every newsletter, and testing tools to build this list. If something is here, it's because people who actually build revenue systems recommended it.

Newsletters

1. Claymation

Weekly GTM engineering newsletter with how-to guides, prompts, and workflows. 6,000+ subscribers. Founded by Alex Lindahl.

2. The GTM Engineer (by Clay)

Official Clay Substack covering building revenue systems with AI and automation.

3. GTMnow (by GTMfund)

Weekly deep-dive on go-to-market strategy from a VC firm's media brand. Curated GTM resources, startups, and job postings.

4. StackOptimise Newsletter

Newsletter from the GTM Engineer Course creators covering signal-based outbound and automation tactics.

Blog covering GTM automation strategies, RevOps playbooks, and tool comparisons.

News, learning, and jobs platform for GTM engineers combining community, careers, and commentary.

4. GTME Pulse OUR PICK

Independent salary data, tool reviews, and career intelligence for GTM engineers.

How We Curated This List

Three criteria. First, does this resource teach you something you can't learn from a Google search? Second, is it actively maintained and producing new content? Third, do practitioners in the role actually recommend it to peers? We don't accept payment for listings. We review and update this page quarterly.

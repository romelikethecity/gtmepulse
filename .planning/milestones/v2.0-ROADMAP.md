## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**
- Decision: Real State of GTME Report 2026 data (n=228) replaces hardcoded estimates (Rationale: First-party survey data is more credible)
- Decision: source_citation_html() pattern for all data pages (Rationale: Establishes authority, consistent attribution)
- Decision: BUILT_*_SLUGS gating for incremental tool/blog publishing (Rationale: Allows partial publishing during multi-plan phases)
- Decision: validate_pages() with 9 QUAL2 categories (Rationale: Automated quality enforcement at build time)
- Decision: Blog articles split into 2 plans (7+7) instead of 1 (Rationale: 14 articles at 1,500-2,500 words each would consume too much context in a single plan)

**Issues Resolved:**
- Replaced all hardcoded salary estimates with real survey data
- Fixed 15 false reframe patterns across 11 pages in quality sweep
- CSS_VERSION management across 4 phases (9 to 14)

**Issues Deferred:**
- FUTURE-01 through FUTURE-06: 30 tool reviews, 8 category indexes, 10 roundups, 10 alternatives, 50 glossary terms, job board page

- Wave 1 phases 1-3 plan checkboxes still unchecked in ROADMAP.md (pre-GSD work)
